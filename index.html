Bexcrest Homes

Established in 2002, Bexcrest Homes is a commercial and residential building contractor and property development business that has built a strong and reputable image based on reliability, quality and attention to detail. Undertaking a multitude of projects, such as refurbishments projects, residential new build projects and conversion projects across London and the Home Counties, Bexcrest Homes has completed projects to the value of £7 million pounds.

Bexcrest Homes has a core of experienced and valuable directly employed personnel, which is complemented by their network of reliable sub-contract partners who have worked together for many a year. The tested and proven relationships have time and time again produced unique projects, suited and engineered to meet the client’s specific design, finish and budget.
