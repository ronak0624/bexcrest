Building Contractor

As a Main Building Contractor Bexcrest Homes can offer all or a portion of the following services to achieve client goals:

Together with our partners we have the capability to offer solutions for demolition needs. These can range from gaining the necessary notices and permissions from local authorities and performing part demolition as a part of an extension/redevelopment project, or complete site demolition for new build properties.
Bexcrest Homes has proven capability to deliver new build residential projects. Together with the client we have meticulously worked together to achieve exactly what was envisaged. Our architects offered various design options relating to the clients needs and visions and gained planning approval from local authorities. Once construction could start our Project Managers assist with all aspects of the build ensure issues were resolved, progress was maintained and the budget adhered to in order to achieve our clients dreams.

Working together with clients to achieve the best end goal, Bexcrest has completed several single storey extensions and double storey side extensions.
Derelict and or redundant buildings are often prime premises to convert into commercial or residential spaces. Bexcrest Homes has worked with clients to convert derelict garages into commercial offices and residential flats, and old conservatories into integral extensions to the existing property.
Bexcrest Homes is proud to have developed a reputation for delivering high quality refurbishments for our clients. Together a clear scope is agreed with realistic budgets and timescales set for completion of the project. Our structured approach addresses all needs and our results have been excellent. We carry out refurbishments by offering our architectural services to design the required changes or follow a set of drawings provided by the client. Either options results in the client being satisfied and the end goal reached.
