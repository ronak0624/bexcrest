Architectural Services

Bexcrest Homes has developed a reliable network of professional and qualified architects who work on a variety of domestic and commercial projects throughout London and the Home Counties. We can offer an entire service package as detailed below or any component for your needs:

  • Free initial site meeting to discuss needs and requirements
  • Our architects will highlight some initial design thoughts outline the range of our services which might be suitable
  • Go through anticipated timelines and associated costs
  • After fee proposal agreement proceed to complete full survey of project site
  • Development of design options for the project
  • Finally agreeing on the right design option
  • Development of CAD design of agreed design option
  • Creation of drawings for submission to Local Planning Authority
  • Development of CGI graphics to enable visualisation of the project
  • Following grant of planning permission prepare and submit application and drawings for Building Control approval prior to project commencement
  • Prior to, during or after Building Regulations approval has been granted, the detailed working drawings for the project can be produce including all structural drawings.
  • Prepare documents from our construction team for a detailed quotation.
  • Following selection of your contractor, we will inspect the works on site, ensuring that they are undertaken to quality, to price, and to time.
  • At the end of the works obtain a certificate of completion from the local authority
